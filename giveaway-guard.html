Boost Your Report Authority

The GiveawayGuard Verified badge increases your trust weight when reporting LARP hosts. Verified reports carry a higher score penalty and are reviewed with priority by our team.

What Verified Members Get
Verified status increases your credibility in the community and gives your reports more weight against LARP hosts.
Gold Verified Badge
Displayed on your profile and next to your reports. Instantly signals you are a credible, accountable community member.
Higher Report Weight
Your LARP reports carry a multiplied trust penalty — up to 2× the score impact compared to unverified reports.
Priority Report Review
Admin reviews your submitted reports first. Verified reports are processed within 12 hours vs. 24 hours for standard reports.
Verified Badge on Reports
Your name appears with a gold badge on every report you file — signalling authenticity to the admin review team.
